## 2. The Core Mechanic — Sell Without Selling

**The rule: TikTok never says "subscribe," "bili," or "bayad." Ever.**

The app already does the selling for us:

1. TikTok/FB video shows a real piece of value from one subject (an explanation, a trick, a code demo).
2. CTA is always some version of: **"Buo 'to sa app — free basahin, link sa bio."**
3. Viewer opens the app, reads the full lesson **for free** (lessons are genuinely free to read).
4. When they try the practice drills, quizzes, and code labs — the part that makes them actually ready — they hit the unlock screen.
5. The unlock screen makes the offer: ₱299, once, entire first year. TikTok never had to.

This means every video is honest ("free nga talaga"), the algorithm doesn't punish us for hard-selling, and the person who pays already experienced the product.

**Framing discipline for the bundle:** Subjects are always presented as *chapters of one survival story* — "kasama 'to sa kit," never "buy this subject." This pushes buyers to the ₱299 bundle instead of ₱49 singles cannibalizing revenue.

## 7. Distribution Playbook

**TikTok mechanics**
- Post 1×/day, **5–7 PM PH time** (after-school/commute scroll). Weekend posts can go 11 AM–1 PM.
- First hour after posting: reply to **every** comment — replies count as engagement velocity and each reply is a mini-post in your voice.
- Pin the comment-bait question (or the best organic comment) on every video.
- Every 3–4 days, make one video that is a **reply to a comment** — TikTok's native format for this gets boosted reach and shows you listen.
- Keep the bio link pointing straight to the app. Bio line: *"Free basahin ang first-year BSIT lessons 👇"*

**Facebook (near-zero extra effort, high-intent audience)**
- Repost every TikTok as an FB Reel the same evening (use the no-watermark export or SnapTik).
- Join 5–10 freshie/enrollment/BSIT groups of PH schools. Post **2–3×/week max** per group, using the 6.5 rewriter output: value-first advice posts, app mentioned only at the end, link only in comments or on request.
- Group posting etiquette: answer other people's questions in those groups even when it has nothing to do with the app. That account becomes known as a helpful senior — that reputation converts better than any post.

**In comments/DMs (where direct answers are allowed)**
- Price questions get the honest frame: "one-time ₱299 for the whole first year, walang expiry — pero basahin mo muna yung free lessons, baka hindi mo pa kailangan."
- That last clause ("baka hindi mo pa kailangan") is deliberate — anti-selling from a kuya reads as honesty and converts better than pushing.

## 8. Metrics & the July 18 Checkpoint

**Daily 5-minute tracker (Google Sheet columns):**
Date | Post topic | Views | Likes | Comments | Profile taps | Bio-link taps | App signups | Sales (₱)

TikTok gives views/likes/comments/profile taps in analytics; bio-link taps from TikTok's link analytics; signups and sales from the admin dashboard.

**Funnel benchmarks to hit ₱8,000** (working backwards, ~3% visitor→sale after reading free lessons):
- ~27 bundle sales ← ~900 app visitors ← ~36 visitors/day ← roughly 1% of daily video views tapping through
- Translation: averaging **~4k views/day across posts** keeps us on pace. With 5k followers and daily posting, this is a normal week — one outlier video changes everything.

**July 18 checkpoint — decision table:**

| Symptom by July 18 | Diagnosis | Action |
|---|---|---|
| < 25k total views for the period | Hooks/format not landing | Shift mix harder toward humor pillar; re-cut Week 1's best performer; study 3 competitor viral posts |
| Views fine, < 20 bio-link taps/day | CTA weak or unclear | Make CTA text-on-screen (not just spoken/caption); add end-frame showing the bio tap |
| Link taps fine, signups but ~0 sales | Paywall value or price friction | **Open the held-back lever:** limited "til-first-day-of-classes" ₱199 bundle promo, announced in-app only (TikTok stays no-sell) |
| Sales happening but slow (~₱150/day) | Working, needs volume | Double down: 2 posts/day on the best-performing format for the final week |

**Hard rule:** don't judge any single video. Judge weeks. The algorithm is noisy; the funnel math only needs one week to compound.

## 9. What NOT to Do (campaign guardrails)

1. **No direct selling on TikTok.** The moment a video says "bili na," reach dies and trust dies with it.
2. **No fake social proof.** Two customers means we show comment engagement, not invented testimonials. When real buyer messages come in, ask permission to screenshot.
3. **No discounting in public.** The ₱199 lever exists only as the July 18 contingency, and only shown in-app.
4. **No skipping days silently.** If a day is truly impossible, post a 10-second reply-to-comment video — anything beats a gap. The algorithm punishes gaps (we've lived this).
5. **No new features this month.** The MVP is good enough — this month the product is the content.
