## 🎯 GTM read

**Three start-of-classes waves define the acquisition calendar:**

1. **June wave** — Silliman (Jun 8). Already in-semester; their **midterms are Sep 1–5** — earliest exam-spike opportunity in the country.
2. **July wave** — Ateneo de Davao (~Jul 14). Freshman onboarding is happening **this week**.
3. **August 10 wave (the big one)** — UP system + USTP + (≈) WVSU + CTU. The bulk of Visayas/Mindanao state-university freshmen enter Aug 4–17. **USTP is confirmed Aug 10** — and USTP students are already 2 of our 10 registered profiles.
4. **September wave** — PUP (~Sep 1), likely MSU system. PUP is our single biggest catchment (Manila) and runs the *latest* calendar: their 1st-sem finals are in **January** and 2nd-sem finals in **June**.

**Implications for the app:**

- **The current traffic slump is seasonal, not fatal.** Most schools are on semester break (June–July). The launch spike (Jun 16) landed at the *end* of AY 2025–26 — the worst possible timing. The real freshman flood arrives **Aug 4 – Sep 1**.
- **Freshman-acquisition campaign window: Jul 20 – Sep 7** (enrollment/orientation season). Target section GCs as they form — that's when block-sale (class-rep) outreach is most natural.
- **First exam-revenue spikes of AY 2026–27:** Silliman midterms Sep 1–5 → WVSU midterms ~Sep 28–Oct 3 → USTP Oct 5–10 → UP mid-sem Oct 7 → PUP midterms ~Oct 19–24. Schedule exam-prep pushes and paywall experiments against these dates.
- **December 9–19** = finals for the whole Aug-10 wave → biggest single conversion window of the semester.
- PUP's June finals mean there is **always some cohort in exam mode** — the exam-calendar content strategy generalizes year-round.
